Budget Management:

1. **Set Monthly Budget**
    1. Define your total spending limit for the month.
2. **Update Monthly Budget**
    1. Adjust your monthly budget as needed.
3. **Track Budget Change History**
    1. Every budget update is recorded with the new value and date.
4. **Set Daily Budget Target**
    1. Break your monthly budget into daily limits to control spending.
5. **Recurring Expenses**
    1. Automatically add repeating costs like rent, subscriptions, or tuition.
6. **Savings Goal Tracker**
    1. Set a monthly savings target and track progress.

Expense Tracking:

1. **Add New Expense**
    1. Enter expense details like name, amount, and optional notes.
2. **Edit or Delete Expense**
    1. Make corrections or remove an expense anytime.
3. **Tag Expenses (Tooltips)**
    1. Use smart tags like `vegetable`, `oil`, `chicken` for quick sorting.
4. **Attach Receipt Image**
    1. Upload or snap a photo of receipts to keep records.
5. **Voice Input**
    1. Add expenses using voice for fast entry.
6. **Shopping List Integration**
    1. Create and compare your to-buy list with actual expenses.
